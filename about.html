About Atlas Explorer

A small, honest guide to how this project works.

Atlas Explorer is a final frontend project — a simple way to look up any country's capital, region, population and a few key facts, all in one place.

Where the data comes from

Two free, public APIs power this site — no account or key needed to browse:

  • Country detailscountries.dev
  • Flag imagesflagcdn.com

Search, filter & sort

On the homepage you can type to search by country or capital name, use the region dropdown to narrow results down to one part of the world, and sort the list by name, population or area — all three update the grid instantly, and can be combined together.

How Favorites work

Tapping the heart icon on any country card saves it to your browser's localStorage — nothing is sent to a server. Your list stays on your device and is still there next time you visit.
